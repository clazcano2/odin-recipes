Sugar Cookies

Description:

These sugar cookies are made to be easily baked and ready to eat out of the oven. They are not only chewy, but also have a nice and sweet texture.

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups margarine
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup white sugar for decoration
