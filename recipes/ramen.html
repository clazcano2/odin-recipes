Ramen

Description:

A nice and hot steaming bowl of ramen that is easy to make and delicious to digest. This is no cup noodles, this is a REAL LIFE bowl of Ichiraku ramen you see in Naruto.

Ingredients:

  • 2 packages of Top Ramen
  • 5 cups of pork/beef broth
  • Pork tenderloin
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 baby bok choy
  • 1 green onion
  • Soy sauce
  • optional: Aburage, Nori, Kamaboko
